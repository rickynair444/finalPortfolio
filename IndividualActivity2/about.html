About Carti's Record Vault

Carti's Record Vault is the place to find the best Carti albums. We have all of his best music and the music that we think is his overall best. Every week we populate this with our weekly favorite Carti music.

Our store is located in the heart of Vampville, LA and is designed to mimic Carti's changing styles.

Our collection includes the best and most tasteful albums/songs that barely anyone else likes.

We also have weekly listening sessions where we blast Playboi Carti's music on huge speakers with a couple subwoofers. These are typically hosted in a rented out venue that we can all be ourselves in.

Our Vault was founded in 2018 by true Carti fans Carti's Record Vault was built strictly from love and passion for the game Our goal is to leave you feeling like you belong with the energy.
