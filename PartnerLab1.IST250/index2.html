About the Designer

Rick Owens, born in 1962, is a designer known for futuristic, dystopian, and minimalist aesthetics. His brand's main goal is to emphasize darkness within luxury streetwear. Some of his works include oversized silhouettes, dark tones, and sometimes even architectural designs.

Rick Owen's Achievements Throughout the Years
  1. Founded his brand in 1994
  2. Relocated branding to Paris in 2003, where he created his brand's workshop
  3. Launched his DRKSHDW line, which was mostly affordable fashion
  4. Collaborated with many fashion brands including Moncler, Birkenstock, and Adidas

His runway shows are known for their raw energy, with models walking in socially bold outfits that redefine the boundaries of contemporary fashion. Many of his works are to symbolize a fusion of art, architecture, and dystopian aesthetics.
