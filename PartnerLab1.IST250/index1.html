Rick Owens

"Beyond Human"

Rick Owens is an American fashion designer based in Paris.
His designs are known for being edgy and rough-hewn,
and are sometimes described as "glunge".

"We all struggle with who we are. We have shame, we have pride, we have self delusion"

Rick Owens' aesthetic is defined by dark, avant-garde fashion that combines architectural silhouettes with monochrome tones. His designs challenge conventional fashion norms, blending luxury with underground aesthetics.

Why do people choose Rick Owens?
  • Bold and futuristic fashion
  • Underground aesthetic mixed beautifully with luxury.
  • Owens takes advantage of the human silhouette in consideration for designs
